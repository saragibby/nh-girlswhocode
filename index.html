Closing the gender gap in tech, one girl at a time.

Computers and technology are everywhere! Everything from the thermostat, to refrigerator, websites to your phone and computer all has code telling it how to work. Making technology & coding jobs the fastest growing in the country, yet girls are being left behind.

Girls Who Code is aiming to bring coding education to high school girls in the New Haven area. Teaching them about Computer Science, and how to write code allowing them to bring all their ideas to life. Opening up doors to career opportunites they never knew possible.

sisterhood

Club girls join a safe and supportive environment of peers & role models and learn to see themselves as computer scientists. Beyond the Club, girls can tap into an alumni network of tens of thousands of girls across the country who are using computer science to solve problems they care about.

code

Club girls learn the concepts of loops, variables, conditionals, and functions that form the basis for all programming languages — whether they want to build a website, an app, or a robot. Returning Clubs girls can deepen their programming knowledge through extended activity sets.

impact

Club girls work in teams to design and build a Computer Science “CS” Impact Project that solves real world problems they care about through code.
